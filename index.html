A little about me

Nice to meet you

It is so good to have you here. Here is a little about me, and the way I work.

I'm Karina Souza, a people and operations professional who brings care, structure, and calm to growing businesses: from team management and recruitment to the operations behind the day to day. My degree is in Human Relations and Organizational Communication. In other words, I studied what I had already been doing my whole working life.

Before the digital world, I spent over eight years working in hospitality, personal assistance, and people operations. I ran the day to day of hotels, busy households, and an executive office, the kind of work where someone's whole routine depends on you getting the details right. It taught me discretion, patience, and how to stay calm when a day falls apart. And it left me with the lesson I still work by: a business runs well when its people do. Human first, organized always.

Since then, I have supported recruitment teams, growing companies, and busy founders through hiring, employee experience, and day-to-day operations. When people are taken care of, everything else follows: performance, loyalty, and growth.
