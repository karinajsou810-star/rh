About me

Teams aren't just hired. They're built — and kept.

I'm Karina Souza, a People Operations & Talent specialist. Companies rarely lose their best people over pay — they lose them because hiring, onboarding, and everyday team life were never built to keep them.

I help growing companies fix that. Across recruitment, onboarding, team coordination, and operations, I've learned that high‑performing teams come down to a few things done consistently well — hiring the right people, getting them productive fast, and building the systems that keep a team together.

I work with startups, consultancies, agencies, and international teams that can't afford to lose the people they worked hard to hire.
