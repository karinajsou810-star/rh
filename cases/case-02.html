The challenge

A recruitment firm losing hours a week managing its own team — instead of recruiting for clients.

For a recruitment firm, the team's time is the product. Every hour spent on internal admin is an hour not used to find candidates, prepare processes, or close vacancies for clients. And absence management — which should be invisible — was consuming time the firm couldn't afford to lose.

The team operated with three contract types simultaneously — permanent, temporary, and external contractors — each with different absence rules and balances calculated differently. Requests came by message, approvals sat in inboxes, and balances were updated manually after each approval. When something went wrong — a wrong balance, a lost approval, coverage not guaranteed — management stopped to fix the problem instead of serving clients.

Employees who don't know how many days they have available lose confidence in the system — and in the company.

"Before, to know how many holiday days I had available, I had to ask someone. And the answer depended on who had the most up-to-date information. That happened to the entire team."

Benedita Souza · Operational team, recruitment firm

The solution

One system. The right rules applied automatically to each contract type.

The firm now has an absence management system built for the reality of a team with mixed contracts: a single flow that automatically applies the correct rules to each type of employee, with no parallel processes and no manual work.

Each employee accesses their balance in real time and submits requests directly in the system. The manager receives the notification, sees the impact on team coverage before deciding, and approves with one click. The balance updates automatically.

Management now anticipates coverage problems instead of reacting to them. The recruitment team returned to the work the firm actually sells: finding the right people.

"We stopped talking about holidays in team meetings. The system handles that. And the management team went back to focusing on what clients hire us for: finding the right people."

Benedita Souza · Operational team, recruitment firm
